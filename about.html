What we do

We provide resources for practitioners looking to improve their inclusivity; a directory of inclusive providers; and a forum for reviewing providers based on respectfulness and inclusivity of their practice.

We are explicitly dedicated to helping folks of all shapes, sizes, and backgrounds find high-quality healthcare. We include but do not limit our scope to: diversity of sexual orientation, gender identity or expression, transgender, non-binary, gender-fluid, and gender-queer; people of size and shape; racial, ethnic, religious, or social background; communities of color; veterans; people with disabilities, both visible and invisible; and mental health and cognitive diversity.
